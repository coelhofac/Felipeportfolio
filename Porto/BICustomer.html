Fiber Company: Providing insights for Customer service improval

Project made entirely in EXCEL, and Tableau.

Results

The goal here was to uncover unproductive customer services by measuring returns between seven days. We should track the center with the highest number and rate of returns. So, in tableau i've created interactive dashboard that allowed our stakeholders to track the metrics and filter it for better insights.

As we presented in the PowerPoint, we see some possible solutions to our request:
Solution 1: Interviewing Market 1 and three manager and funcionarios for getting insights and difficulties. Solution 2: Propose for the Best Market Performance's Manager to help the worst team for a determined period, as two weeks, and at the end, retrieve a report that could help stakeholders and data team in further analysis.
